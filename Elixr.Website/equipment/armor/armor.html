Armor

Armor is worn by characters to increase their Defense, making them less likely to be hit. Armor descriptions include: Bestows Disadvantage, Speed Penalty, and Don Time.

Bestows Disadvantage

Wearing Armor that is 45 pounds or more causes some skills to have Disadvantage due to the armor's awkwardness. These skills include: Athletics, Acrobatics, Climb, Swim, and Stealth

Speed Penalty

Some Armor is hard to move in. While wearing such Armor, your Movement Speed is reduced by the Speed Penalty.

Don Time

Armor takes time to put on. The Don Time is how many rounds it takes to fully put on armor.

The time it takes to remove armor is half of the Don Time.
